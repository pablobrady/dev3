Liquid Rock

A responsive website for a Bay Area rock band
  • Features deep integration with Google Calendar JSON, Flicker images integration, an embedded (but responsive) YouTube video player, and a booking contact form
  • DESIGNED the website and the logo

My Tech:
HTML5, Sass, JS, jQuery, PHP, Apache II, NPM, Git, Heroku, AJAX subpages for a single-page design

Biggest Challenge:
I made this site single-paged and "AngularJS-like", because if a visitor started playing a music audio sample, I wanted the music to keep playing no matter where the vistor went on the site.

But because a band member owns other PHP-based band websites, I skipped AngularJS and chose PHP to minimize any future development efforts for them.
